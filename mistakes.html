Everyday Mistakes That Cause Plumbing Problems

Many everyday habits can contribute to plumbing issues over time. These points focus on awareness and prevention rather than repair.

Most Common Plumbing Mistakes

  • Flushing wipes or sanitary products: Even “flushable” wipes can cause blockages.
  • Pouring grease or cooking oil down the sink: Grease solidifies and blocks pipes.
  • Ignoring slow drainage or gurgling sounds: Early warning signs often lead to bigger issues.
  • Allowing limescale to build up: Hard water in Marlow makes this especially common.
  • Not knowing where the main stop valve is: This can delay action during leaks or emergencies.
  • Over-tightening tap handles or fixtures: This can damage seals and threads.

Source: “Flushing wipes… pouring grease… ignoring slow drainage… limescale… stop valve… over-tightening fixtures.”

How Avoiding These Mistakes Helps

By avoiding these common habits, homeowners can reduce the likelihood of blockages, leaks and unnecessary wear on their plumbing systems. Awareness is often the first step in preventing costly repairs.
