Welcome

This website explains how typical plumbing systems work in Marlow. It covers how water enters a property, how it is distributed, how waste water leaves, and how local conditions such as hard water and property age can influence performance.

The goal is to provide clear, neutral information that helps residents understand the systems in their homes and recognise common patterns or symptoms.

What You Can Learn Here

Water supply

How mains water reaches homes, how pressure varies across the area, and what the main stop valve does.

Drainage

How waste water leaves the property, the role of traps and vents, and why blockages occur.

Water hardness

Why Marlow has very hard water, how limescale forms, and how it affects appliances and fittings.

Common issues

Typical symptoms such as low pressure, slow drains, noisy pipes and visible limescale, and what they may indicate.
