Common Questions

What causes low water pressure in Marlow?

Low water pressure is often caused by limescale buildup, partially closed valves, or supply issues from the water provider. Homes at higher elevations may also notice weaker flow.

Why do drains block so easily?

Drains commonly block due to grease, wipes, hair and food waste entering the system. Older clay pipes and tree roots can also contribute in some areas.

How does a combi boiler work?

A combi boiler heats water on demand without a storage tank. Any work on boilers or gas appliances must be carried out by qualified professionals.

Where is the main stop valve located?

The main stop valve is usually found under the kitchen sink, in a utility cupboard, or outside near the property boundary.
